I am particularly drawn to problems that require a blend of rigorous reasoning, computation, and experimentation, especially in areas such as Ramsey theory, graph generation, statistical computing, and the mathematical foundations of cryptographic protocols.

This site will gradually bring together my academic background, research directions, technical notes, computational explorations, selected projects, and related teaching and contact information.
